## Survival Matrix

| Phenomenon | Paper-backed claim | Survives in repo as | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Gravity-related collapse time | `tau ~ hbar / E_Delta` as the conjectured rate | `supported_hypothesis` | The rate relation is part of the Diósi/Penrose proposal, not an established fact. |
| Decoherence objection | Brain/microtubule coherence times are far shorter in Tegmark's calculation | `supported_objection` | Useful as a hard counterclaim; remains a key negative benchmark. |
| Decoherence rebuttal | Orch-OR parameter corrections can extend the timescale into `10^-5` to `10^-4` s in the 2000 reply | `contested_support` | This is a rebuttal, not closure. |
| Gravity collapse bounds | Underground tests rule out the natural parameter-free DP version | `supported_constraint` | Useful for bounding parameter ranges, not for validating consciousness. |
| Microtubule energy transport | Electronic energy can diffuse ~6.6 nm and anesthetics reduce exciton diffusion | `supported_microphysics` | This is the strongest microtubule-adjacent result currently in the repo. |
| Tryptophan superradiance | Organized tryptophan networks show enhanced UV superradiance and fluorescence quantum yield | `supported_microphysics` | Good for photophysics, not proof of awareness. |
| Microtubule structural dynamics | Cracks between protofilaments influence growth/shrinkage transitions | `supported_structure` | Useful for showing MTs are dynamic and structured, not static scaffolds. |
| Gap junction oscillation control | Gap-junction plasticity can regulate network-wide oscillations | `supported_network_biology` | Supports the existence of network-scale synchronization channels, but not quantum tunneling claims. |
| Consciousness from Orch-OR | Conscious experience arises from microtubule OR events | `unproven_interpretation` | Keep as the model claim, clearly labeled. |
| Anesthetic mechanism | Anesthetics quench tubulin coherence as the direct cause of unconsciousness | `unproven_interpretation` | The repo should treat this as a hypothesis awaiting discriminating tests. |

## Repo Surviving Parts

The following components remain in the repo because the papers support them at least as diagnostics or strong hypotheses:

1. `tau = hbar / E_G` as a model equation.
2. Decoherence range comparisons between Tegmark and Hagan/Hameroff/Tuszyński.
3. DP lower-bound / parameter sensitivity tooling.
4. Microtubule energy-transport and superradiance notes.
5. Microtubule structural-dynamics and gap-junction notes as adjacent support.
6. Anesthetic perturbation tables framed as model predictions, not proof.

## Parts That Stay Flagged

1. Any statement that microtubules are proven conscious substrates.
2. Any statement that 613 THz oscillations are a settled neural clock.
3. Any statement that the quantum nudge is quantitatively established without a derivation.
4. Any statement that Orch-OR is validated by current evidence.
